Amazon Protocol

Sinopse

An international crisis over Amazon sovereignty erupts into something far deadlier than anyone imagined.
When Brazilian Special Forces launch a top-secret operation deep in the rainforest, they're prepared for enemy fire—not the nightmare that awaits them in the green depths. Led by a hardened military commander, the elite unit faces horrors that push them beyond duty into a desperate fight for survival.

But escaping the Amazon alive is only the first challenge. When Lieutenant Ramos and his team emerge from weeks of isolation, they discover a world turned upside down: international forces have moved to occupy the Amazon basin, and Brazil's sovereignty hangs by a thread.

Caught between foreign invaders and a government conspiracy, Lieutenant Ramos must navigate a web of betrayal that reaches the highest levels of power. As he uncovers the true scope of the threat, he realizes the Amazon crisis is just the beginning—and the survival of humanity itself may depend on choices he makes in the coming hours.
In the heart of the Amazon, some secrets are worth dying for. Others are worth killing for.
