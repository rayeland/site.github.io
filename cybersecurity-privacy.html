Cybersecurity and Privacy For Everyone: Protecting Your Digital Life from Online Threats

Synopsis

Welcome to the 2026 edition. When we published our first edition in 2019, the digital world was an entirely different place. Today, in just seven years, we have witnessed a silent revolution that has completely redefined our interaction with technology. The smartphones in our pockets already allow us to interact with Artificial Intelligence at any given moment. Cloud computing has ceased to be an option, becoming the new standard and fundamentally transforming the way we store, process, and share data.


It is within this context that the threats posed by governments and other criminals take on epic proportions. The theft of identity, crypto assets, bank funds, or personal data has become even easier. Criminals have also begun leveraging all these remarkable resources as tools to take advantage of unprepared individuals and companies. The good news is this: if you do not want to be the next victim, a few simple measures and minor changes in your habits can prevent massive financial losses and severe headaches.


This book is your companion in a world where digital security has become more crucial than ever before. Our goal is to provide you with the practical knowledge necessary to maintain your safety, even in the face of mounting challenges posed by criminals operating within the digital realm. This work was designed to guide you through this ever-shifting technological landscape. Here, you will find not only technical information, but also practical strategies and actionable advice to protect your privacy and elevate your overall security.
